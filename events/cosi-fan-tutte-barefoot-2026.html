About the Production

Oscar Castellino joins Barefoot Opera for their fully-staged production of Mozart's Così fan tutte, performed as an intimate physical-theatre opera condensed into two vivid 50-minute halves and sung in the original Italian.

The production is directed by Jenny Miller, with musical direction by Lesley-Anne Sammons, and coached by Satoshi Kubo and vocal specialist Penny Randall Davis. Following an intensive April rehearsal week in South London, the cast performs across a summer and autumn tour spanning London and the South East.
