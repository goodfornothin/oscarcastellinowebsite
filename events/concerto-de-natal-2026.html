About This Concert

Oscar Castellino's annual Concerto de Natal (Christmas Concert) has become a beloved tradition, bringing together the magic of the holiday season with world-class operatic performance. This festive evening features a carefully curated program of opera arias, beloved carols, and seasonal favorites.

From the grandeur of Handel's Messiah to the warmth of traditional Christmas carols, Oscar creates an intimate atmosphere where audiences can celebrate the spirit of the season through music.
