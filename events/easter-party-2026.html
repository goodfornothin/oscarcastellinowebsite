About the Event

A Night to Remember

Lo'Mas Event returns this Easter Sunday with an unforgettable night of celebration. Party & Celebrations 2026 brings together some of the finest musical talent for an evening that spans opera, ballroom, Afro house, and high-energy dance.

Featuring a special live performance by baritone Oscar Castellino, along with an ensemble of DJs, musicians and an MC to guide you through the evening — this is a night for the whole family, from the youngest to the eldest.

Enjoy a full buffet dinner, family entertainment, a photo booth, and much more, all in the beautiful surroundings of The Bull Hotel.

Location

The Venue

The Bull Hotel

Oxford Road, Gerrards Cross, Buckinghamshire, SL9 7PA

Set in the heart of Gerrards Cross, The Bull Hotel is a beautifully appointed venue with a rich history, offering elegant event spaces, ample parking, and easy access from London and the surrounding areas.

  • 🚂
    By Train Gerrards Cross station (Chiltern Railways from London Marylebone, approx. 22 minutes). The Bull Hotel is a short 3-minute walk from the station exit.
  • 🚗
    By Car Exit the M40 at Junction 1 or the M25 at Junction 16, following signs for Gerrards Cross (A413). Free on-site parking is available at the venue.
  • 🚌
    By Bus Local bus routes connect Gerrards Cross to Uxbridge, Slough and Beaconsfield. Alight at Gerrards Cross town centre.
