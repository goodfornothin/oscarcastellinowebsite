About the Concert

A Celebration of Goa's Musical Heritage

Following the grand success of Goan Interactions 2025, the Baluji Music Foundation in collaboration with Melville Rodrigues and Oscar Castellino bring to you an enchanting evening of timeless Goan Konkani and Portuguese tunes.

Celebrate Goa's unique European-Indian musical tapestry in a countryside-like London setting featuring an exceptional ensemble of world-class musicians. Authentic Goan snacks will be available during the interval.

The Story

The History of Goan Interactions

Goan Interactions was devised by Linda Shanson and Baluji Shrivastav OBE from the Baluji Music Foundation, which in addition to championing music by blind musicians has a special focus on music from South Asia. They wanted to give a platform to Goan music that is largely unexplored in the West.

They collaborated with esteemed opera soprano Patricia Rozario and pianist Mark Troop for the first concert in 2021. Patricia and Mark programmed the concert musically for the first few editions. Baluji Shrivastav OBE added a special touch with his expertise in Indian classical music, assisted by Yusuf Ali Khan on percussion.

Oscar Castellino, who featured as a singer in the early editions, then took up programming the concert in 2025. Mark Troop, who took a break from the concert in 2025 due to an international tour, rejoins in 2026 playing a pivotal role in programming with his vast experience arranging Goan music.
