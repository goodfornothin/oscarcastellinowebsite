About This Event

Oscar Castellino brings his Goan heritage to life in this special evening celebrating the rich musical traditions of Goa through traditional Konkani songs, mandos, and dulpods.

Born to a Goan family with roots tracing to the vibrant coastal villages of Goa, Oscar has made it his mission to preserve and share the beautiful musical heritage of his ancestors. This concert features beloved Konkani songs passed down through generations, performed with the passion and authenticity that only a true son of Goa can deliver.
